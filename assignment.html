Culture

Culture is the shared beliefs, traditions, customs, and values of a group or society.It shapes how people live, communicate, celebrate, and express themselves.

Lifestyle

Lifestyle is the way an individual or group lives, based on daily habits and choices.It includes diet, work, hobbies, fashion, and health routines.

Culture and lifestyle together reflect the shared values, traditions, and daily habits that shape how individuals and communities live.
